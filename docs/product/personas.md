# User Personas

Three personas, each stress-testing a different axis of the product. Frustrations and the broader problem are in [the PRD](./prd.md).

## Marie — The Beginner

New to genealogy, moderate technical comfort. A cousin handed her a GEDCOM file and she wants to explore it and add her immediate family without a steep learning curve. Marie stress-tests **first-session usability**: GEDCOM import must work on day one, and the UI must stay legible to someone who has never used genealogy software.

## Robert — The Enthusiast

15+ years of research, a tree of 2,000+ individuals across 10 generations, regularly exchanging GEDCOM with other researchers and archives. He has lost data to a crash before. Robert stress-tests **scale and integrity**: responsive CRUD on large trees, and GEDCOM round-trip with zero data loss or silent duplication.

## Claire — The Researcher

10 years of methodical, source-driven work — every fact backed by evidence and a document. Claire stress-tests the **source-first workflow**: sources, citations, and repositories as first-class entities, file attachments linked to entities, and GEDCOM export that preserves all of it.
